I'm a 3rd year PhD student, DOE Computational Science Graduate Fellow (2018-2022), and QuICS Lanczos Graduate Fellow (2018-2020) in theoretical physics at the University of Maryland, College Park. I'm also affiliated with the Joint Quantum Institute (JQI) and the Joint Center for Quantum Information and Computer Science (QuICS). My advisor is Alexey Gorshkov.

Research

I'm interested in quantum algorithms and mathematical physics. Current areas of research include quantum adiabatic computation and quantum sensing.
